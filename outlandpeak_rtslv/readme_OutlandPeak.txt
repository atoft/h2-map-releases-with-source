                   ===Outland Peak===
An entry to the RunThinkShootLiveVille level design contest,
created by Alastair Toft aka MisterAddy.

The map includes custom material and texture files, which I
created myself.
                          ===
Setting: An old cable-car station high in the White Forest
mountains has become the foundation for a Combine listening
post. Infiltrate the outpost and obtain vital intelligence.

Spoilers follow:
RUN: Flee from a chasing Antlion Guard.
THINK: Find a way into the Combine outpost.
SHOOT: Survive combat with Combine soldiers.
LIVE: Escape the mountain...
